Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Your trust is our priority. Learn how Apply Assistant secures your job hunting files, profile details, and localized application records.

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

1. Overview

Welcome to Apply Assistant. We operate a local-first mobile job application tracking tool designed to centralize scattered job posts, log application progress, perform CV gaps analysis, generate cover letters, and help organize your job-hunting workflow.

Because we value your privacy, our core architecture is designed to store data directly on your device by default, utilizing local offline SQLite databases (Drift). Cloud services or external API processing are used strictly when you configure and trigger features (like syncing data to Supabase or tailoring documents with Google Gemini APIs). All data transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption.

Our Privacy Commitments
  • Local-First Control: Your job pipeline, resumes, CV profiles, tailored cover letters, and notes are stored exclusively on your physical device. We do not sync, store, or backup this data on any cloud servers.
  • No Data Monetization: We do not monitor, sell, distribute, or monetize your CVs, resumes, or search targets.
  • No Background Email Access: The application does not connect to your personal email accounts or parse your private email inbox.

2. Data We Process

The app processes the following categories of information to manage your job application tracker and generate career documents:

Account Details
If you register an account (powered securely by Supabase), we store your email address, password hash, and optional profile metadata (such as your full name and phone number).
Job Application Pipeline
Stored strictly locally on your device. This includes job titles, employer names, job descriptions, URLs, locations, notes, and application status. If you paste messy text, local or cloud parsers extract structured fields.
Resumes & CV Profiles
Stored strictly locally on your device. Your CV profiles, raw text resumes, and details like skills, work history, and education. This information is utilized for CV gap analyses and cover letter generation.
Manual Tracking Inputs
The contact email address of the employer, the date you marked the job as applied, custom follow-up tallies, and manual log notes for the application.

3. Manual Email Tracking & No Server Integrations

Unlike automatic email aggregators, Apply Assistant does not sync with your inbox, ask for Google OAuth permission, or link to your email server. All email-related tracking in the app is entirely manual:

How Manual Tracking Works
  • Recruiter Email Contact: You can optionally type the recruiter's email address manually when marking a job as "Applied". This email address is stored locally for reference and is not shared.
  • Local 14-Day Timer: The 14-day closure loop runs on local scheduled notifications and calendar intervals. No emails are scanned to verify replies.
  • Manual Status Updates: You update the status of your application (e.g. marking as "Replied", "Ghosted", or "Interviewing") manually in the tracking UI.

4. AI & Document Services Details

Apply Assistant supports advanced AI-driven features like resume gap analysis, CV keyword injection, tailored cover letter generation, and LaTeX CV exporting. These functions process your career text and job requirements as follows:

Google Gemini API via Supabase Edge Function Proxy

When you trigger CV Gap Analysis or Cover Letter Tailoring, the app securely transmits the job description and your resume profile to our Supabase Edge Function gateway. This gateway secures all communications and features an automatic model fallback chain to handle rate limits, service unavailability, or model errors. The gateway dynamically tests and routes requests through verified model slugs in the following sequence: gemini-2.5-flash → gemini-3.1-flash-lite → gemma-4-26b-a4b-it → gemma-4-31b-it → gemini-3-flash-preview. Transmissions are encrypted using TLS 1.3, and data is processed strictly to generate responses; your inputs are not used to train the underlying models.

LaTeX Document Compilation (Google Cloud Run)

If you choose to export your tailored CV as a professional LaTeX PDF, the app compiles the LaTeX code using a state-free compiler hosted on Google Cloud Run. The raw LaTeX source is sent encrypted via HTTPS/TLS 1.3 to the compiler endpoint, where it is compiled directly in-memory. The compiled PDF file bytes are returned to your device. No document files, resume data, or personal details are permanently stored, cached, or logged on the compiler server.

On-Device Offline Engine Fallback

If no internet connection is available or if you operate in offline mode, the app bypasses all cloud models entirely. Instead, it processes CVs and templates using a local, rule-based keyword scanner and template engine running 100% on your device, ensuring no external data transmission takes place.

5. Storage, Security & Notifications

We use secure local storage and local scheduling engines to manage your job application logs and career documents, along with cloud-based authentication for user profiles:

  • Local SQLite Storage (Drift): Your job pipelines, resumes, CV profiles, tailored cover letters, and application logs are saved directly to an offline-first SQLite database inside sandboxed mobile directories that cannot be accessed by other applications. This data is stored strictly on your device and is not synchronized to any cloud servers.
  • Supabase Authentication & Profile: If you register an account, we use Supabase to store your authentication details (email and password hash) and profile metadata (full name and phone number) securely. No resume documents, cover letters, or job application tracker tables are uploaded or synchronized to Supabase.
  • Secure Token Storage: Any authentication keys or session tokens are kept in secure local keychains (iOS Keychain and Android Keystore).
  • Local Notifications (Awesome Notifications): To schedule follow-up reminders (7 days after applying) and closure warnings (13 days after applying), the app utilizes the awesome_notifications local engine. These alarms are scheduled entirely on-device and run locally without communicating with external push servers. This feature requires your explicit system notification permission.

6. Data Deletion & User Control

You have absolute control over your private job search data. A permanent account deletion option is built directly into the app:

Permanent Data Deletion

Under Settings → Danger Zone → Delete My Data, users can trigger an automated deletion process which executes immediately:

  • Local Storage Wipe: Deletes all local Drift SQLite tables (jobs, applications, CV profiles, and local notes).
  • Cloud Deletion: Permanently deletes your user authentication record and profile metadata from Supabase.
  • App Cache Removal: Wipes all local shared preferences, cache directories, and exported PDF documents on your device.

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
